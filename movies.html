The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

The Intern (2015)

"The Intern" is a heartwarming comedy-drama starring Robert De Niro as a 70-year-old widower who becomes an intern at an online fashion retailer. The film explores themes of mentorship, friendship, and personal growth with humor and warmth.

The Social Network (2010)

Directed by David Fincher, "The Social Network" tells the story of the founding of Facebook by Mark Zuckerberg and the legal battles that followed. With a gripping screenplay by Aaron Sorkin, the film explores themes of ambition, betrayal, and innovation in the digital age.

King Richard (2021)

"King Richard" is a biographical drama following Richard Williams, the determined father of tennis legends Venus and Serena Williams. The film showcases his unwavering belief in his daughters' potential and his extraordinary efforts to shape them into world champions.
